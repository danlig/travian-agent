# Buildings and Resource Fields Statistics

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000090158-buildings-and-resource-fields-statistics
Sezione origine: Basic Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

Buildings and resource fields form the foundation of your village. They determine how you produce and store resources, train troops, defend your settlement, and expand your empire.

Resource fields are located outside your village walls and generate the core resources needed for growth. Buildings in the village center serve different purposes. Some increase or support resource production, others allow you to trade, join alliances, settle new villages, or prepare for war by training and improving troops. This overview includes all buildings and resource fields along with details about their construction costs, effects, and the requirements needed to build them.

At the top of the tool, you can adjust settings such as game speed and Main Building level. Any changes will update the displayed values immediately.

Inside each building table, you can click to select specific levels. The tool will then show the total cost and time at the bottom, which helps you plan upgrades such as checking how long it takes to improve a building from level 10 to level 15.

## Implicazioni strategiche
- Gestisci risorse, produzione e capienza come vincoli principali: spendi prima di raccogliere reward grandi e usa NPC merchant solo quando sblocca timing o previene overflow significativo.
- Ogni scelta militare deve avere ritorno chiaro: profitto, difesa, conquista, demolizione mirata o pressione diplomatica.
- Diplomazia e informazione riducono rischio: controlla messaggi/report, comunica naturalmente e costruisci affidabilita.
- Pianifica territorio e cultura in anticipo: capitale, cropper, oasi e slot espansione decidono la traiettoria mid game.
