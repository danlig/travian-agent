# Buildings And Resource Fields Statistics

Source: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000090158-buildings-and-resource-fields-statistics

Resource fields produce the core resources. Village-center buildings support storage, trade, alliances, expansion, defense, and troop production.

Operational notes:
- Resource fields are the foundation of early account growth.
- Storage capacity must be considered before collecting rewards.
- Building time depends on game speed and Main Building level.
- Plan upgrades by total cost, time, and production effect.
- Early field balance matters because many future upgrades have uneven resource requirements.
