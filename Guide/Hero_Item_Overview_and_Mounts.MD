# Hero Item Overview and Mounts

Source: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000064021-hero-item-overview-and-mounts
Source section: Advanced Gameplay

## What It Teaches

Hero items can strengthen your hero and provide useful bonuses during gameplay. These bonuses either benefit the hero directly or affect troops fighting together with the hero, if they are from the same home village as the hero.

To learn more about specific item categories, you can check these articles:

- Right-Hand Items
- Armour: Helmets, Chest Armour, Boots
- Consumables

## Mounts

Mounts increase your hero’s speed. They do not increase the speed of accompanying troops. Whenever the hero travels with an army, the army travels at the speed of the slowest unit, including the hero.

Below you will find the available mounts and their effects on different server speeds.

### Gelding (Tier 1)

Provides a speed boost for the hero only.
- 1x servers: 14 fields/hour
- 3x servers: 28 fields/hour

### Thoroughbred (Tier 2)

Provides a higher speed boost for the hero only.
- 1x servers: 17 fields/hour
- 3x servers: 34 fields/hour

### Warhorse (Tier 3)

Provides the highest speed boost for the hero only.
- 1x servers: 20 fields/hour
- 3x servers: 40 fields/hour

## Strategic Implications
- Manage resources, production, and storage capacity as your main constraints: spend before collecting large rewards, and use the NPC merchant only when it unlocks better timing or prevents significant overflow.
- Your hero and items are accelerators: take safe adventures, keep gear aligned with your current goal, and preserve health to avoid downtime.
- Every military decision must have a clear payoff: profit, defense, conquest, targeted demolition, or diplomatic pressure.
- Plan territory and culture ahead of time: capital, cropper, oases, and expansion slots determine your mid-game trajectory.
