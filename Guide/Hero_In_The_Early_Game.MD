# Hero In The Early Game

Source: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000092525-hero-in-the-early-game

The hero is an economic engine early because resource-production points compound every hour. Keeping the hero alive matters more than risky combat.

Operational notes:
- Prefer resource-production hero points early.
- Send normal/short adventures while health is high; avoid hard adventures if health is low or no ointments are available.
- First adventure rewards are predictable enough to justify active completion, but every death delays growth.
- Monitor hero health after each adventure before sending the next one.
- Early rewards include resources, troops, silver, ointment, book of wisdom, and later cages.
