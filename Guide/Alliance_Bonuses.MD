# Alliance Bonuses

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000060369-alliance-bonuses
Sezione origine: Advanced Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

Alliance bonuses allow alliance members to collectively improve important game mechanics by donating resources. Once unlocked, bonuses apply to all alliance members, with some limitations for newly joined players. This article explains how alliance bonuses work, what effects they provide, how to unlock them, and which exceptions apply.

## Overview of Alliance Bonuses

Alliances can unlock four different bonuses. Each bonus has five levels, and higher levels increase the effect of that bonus.

The available alliance bonuses are:

### Recruitment
- Increases troop production speed in all troop-producing buildings

### Philosophy
- Increases Culture Point (CP) production
- CP is generated by buildings and hero helmets
- Town Hall celebrations are not affected, except on specific special servers

### Metallurgy
- Increases troop offensive and defensive strength
- This bonus is applied on top of existing smithy upgrades

### Commerce
- Increases merchant carrying capacity

## Special Server Exceptions

On some special servers, alliance bonuses behave differently.

### Annual Special 2024 / Northern Legends
- Philosophy bonus also affects Town Hall celebrations and artworks
- The CP limit from these sources is increased
- Commerce bonus increases merchant speed in addition to carrying capacity

## Bonus Levels and Effects

Each bonus increases gradually with its level. The effects are identical across all four bonus types.

```text
Level Effect
0 No bonus
1 +2% / +4%
2 +4% / +8%
3 +6% / +12%
4 +8% / +16%
5 (max) +10% / +20%
```

(The exact percentage depends on the bonus type.)

## Unlocking Alliance Bonuses

Alliance bonuses are unlocked through resource donations from alliance members.

Key rules:
- Each bonus level requires a higher total amount of resources
- All four bonuses have the same resource requirements and upgrade times
- Resource type does not matter; only the total amount donated is counted
- Resources cannot be donated while a level is actively unlocking

Once the required amount is reached, the upgrade countdown begins automatically.

### Upgrade Times and Donation Limits

```text
Level Upgrade Time* Required Donations Daily Donation Limit*
1 24 hours 1,200,000 300,000
2 48 hours 5,600,000 400,000
3 72 hours 17,100,000 550,000
4 96 hours 51,200,000 750,000
5 120 hours 153,600,000 1,000,000
```

* Upgrade time and donation limits are adjusted by server speed.

### Donation Limits
- Each player has a daily donation limit
- The limit depends on the highest bonus level currently unlocked in the alliance
- Donation limits cannot be exceeded

For 3 Gold, players can triple a donation (e.g. 10,000 counts as 30,000), but this still cannot exceed the daily donation limit.

## Interaction With Other Game Mechanics

Some alliance bonuses are multiplied with other effects:
- Recruitment bonus is multiplied with hero items (e.g. Helmet of the Mercenary)
- Metallurgy bonus is multiplied with smithy upgrades
- Commerce bonus is multiplied with the Trade Office

### Commerce Example
- Gaul merchant base capacity: 750
- Level 4 Commerce bonus (120%): 750 × 2.2 = 1,650
- Level 20 Trade Office (400%): 750 × 5 = 3,750
- Combined effect: 750 × 2.2 × 5 = 8,250

## Daily Quests and Alliance Bonuses

Alliance contributions are part of daily quests.
- While the last alliance bonus is unlocking, the daily quest cannot be completed
- Once all alliance bonuses finish activating, the daily quest is automatically marked as completed for all alliance members each day

## Availability for Newly Joined Players

Alliance bonuses are not fully available immediately to new members.

```text
Bonus Level Available After Joining
Level 0 Immediately
Level 1 Immediately
Level 2 24 hours
Level 3 48 hours
Level 4 72 hours
Level 5 96 hours
```

(Availability timers are affected by server speed.)

## Implicazioni strategiche
- Gestisci risorse, produzione e capienza come vincoli principali: spendi prima di raccogliere reward grandi e usa NPC merchant solo quando sblocca timing o previene overflow significativo.
- L'eroe e gli oggetti sono acceleratori: avventure sicure, equip coerente con obiettivo corrente e salute preservata per evitare downtime.
- Ogni scelta militare deve avere ritorno chiaro: profitto, difesa, conquista, demolizione mirata o pressione diplomatica.
- Diplomazia e informazione riducono rischio: controlla messaggi/report, comunica naturalmente e costruisci affidabilita.
- Pianifica territorio e cultura in anticipo: capitale, cropper, oasi e slot espansione decidono la traiettoria mid game.
- Adatta stile e priorita alla tribu: sfrutta bonus naturali invece di seguire una build generica.
