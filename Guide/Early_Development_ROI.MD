# Early Development: Return on Investment

Source: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000091814-early-development-return-on-investment-roi-

ROI measures how fast an investment pays for itself: total resources spent divided by extra resources gained per hour. Lower payback time is better.

Operational notes:
- In the opening, prioritize resource fields and upgrades that increase hourly production fastest.
- Compare field upgrades by cost versus marginal production gain, not only by current resource balance.
- Avoid early troop training unless farming income can support it; otherwise economy and second-village progress slow down.
- Expansion usually beats expensive late building/oasis investments when the new village produces resources and culture points quickly.
- Use quest rewards strategically: claim only when storage can hold them and spend them into high-ROI growth.
