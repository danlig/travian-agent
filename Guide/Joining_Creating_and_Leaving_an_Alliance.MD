# Joining, Creating and Leaving an Alliance

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000060449-joining-creating-and-leaving-an-alliance
Sezione origine: Advanced Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

Alliances are a core part of advanced gameplay in Travian: Legends. This article explains how to join an alliance, how to create one, how invitations work, and when and how you can leave an alliance. The information below follows the in-game rules and mechanics exactly and is intended to help you resolve common questions without contacting support.

## Joining an Alliance

To join an alliance, you must meet both of the following conditions:
- You have built an Embassy level 1
- You have received an alliance invitation from that alliance

### Finding Alliances Near You

Once your Embassy is at least level 1, you can view nearby alliances:
- Open your Embassy
- Go to the Find alliance tab
- Review the list of local alliances in your area

From this list, you can click an alliance name to view its leaders. You may contact them directly via message to ask whether they are willing to invite you.

### Accepting an Alliance Invitation

If an alliance leader sends you an invitation:
- You will receive a message from the Ambassador
- The invitation will appear inside your Embassy

To join the alliance, open your Embassy and accept the invitation there. If you do not yet have an Embassy, you must build one before you can accept the invite.

## Creating an Alliance

You can create your own alliance once your Embassy reaches level 3.

To create an alliance:
- Open your Embassy
- Select Found Alliance
- Enter an alliance tag (short name)
- Enter the full alliance name
- Save your changes

Once created, you become the alliance founder and receive full alliance rights by default.

## Sending Alliance Invitations

Only players with the appropriate rights can invite others to an alliance:
- The alliance founder (default full rights)
- Members who have been explicitly granted permission to invite players

To send an invitation:
- Open the alliance interface
- Enter the player’s name exactly as it appears in-game
- Confirm the invitation

If the invitation is sent successfully:
- The message “An invitation has been sent to …” will be displayed
- The invitation will appear in the alliance Invitations list

Once the invited player accepts the invitation, they will immediately become a member of the alliance.

## Leaving an Alliance

There are specific rules regarding when you can leave an alliance:
- You can leave an alliance no earlier than 24 hours after joining
- During this initial 24-hour period, you also cannot be kicked from the alliance

After this protection period has passed, you may leave the alliance at any time:
- Open the Alliance interface
- Go to the Options tab
- Select Quit alliance

Leaving an alliance does not require approval from alliance leadership.
