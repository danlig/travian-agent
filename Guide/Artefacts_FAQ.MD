# Artefacts FAQ

Source: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000092460-artefacts-faq
Source section: Game Guides

## What It Teaches

## Do artefact effects stack?

No. Artefact effects of the same type do not stack.

If you own multiple artefacts with the same effect:
- A village-scope artefact overrides an account-scope artefact for that specific village
- All other villages continue to use the account-scope artefact

Example: If you own a Faster Troops artefact with village scope and another with account scope:
- The village holding the village-scope artefact uses its own bonus
- All other villages use the account-scope bonus

## Does the “Stronger Buildings” artefact affect walls?

Yes. The Stronger Buildings artefact also applies to walls.

## How many artefacts can I own and use?

You can own any number of artefacts on your account.

However, at any given time:
- A maximum of three artefacts can be active
- Only one account-scope artefact can be active

If multiple artefacts conflict:
- Village-scope artefacts override account-scope artefacts of the same effect
- Effects of the same type do not stack

## Do unique artefacts override small artefacts?

No.

Unique artefacts are considered account-scope artefacts. Even if a unique artefact has a stronger effect, a small (village-scope) artefact still overrides it in that village.

Example:
- Unique Eyes (×10, account-scope)
- Small Eyes (×5, village-scope)

Scouting operations sent from the village with Small Eyes will use the ×5 effect, not the unique one.

## What happens if my Artefact of the Fool changes to account scope?

If the Artefact of the Fool changes its scope to account-wide and you already own another account-scope artefact:
- The order in which artefacts were obtained matters
- The artefact acquired earlier has priority

## I conquered a fourth artefact. Will it activate?

Yes, depending on scope and acquisition order.

Rules:
- The oldest account-scope artefact can be active
- The two oldest village-scope artefacts can be active
- If no account-scope artefact exists, the three oldest village-scope artefacts can be active

After the activation delay, the correct artefacts will automatically become active.

## I already have two account-scope artefacts and one village-scope artefact. What happens if I conquer another village-scope artefact?

After activation:
- One account-scope artefact
- Two village-scope artefacts

will be active at the same time.

## Does an artefact travel with my hero or troops after capture?

No. Once capture conditions are met, artefacts teleport instantly to the appropriate treasury. They do not physically travel with troops or heroes.

## Strategic Implications
- Your hero and items are accelerators: take safe adventures, keep gear aligned with your current goal, and preserve health to avoid downtime.
- Every military decision must have a clear payoff: profit, defense, conquest, targeted demolition, or diplomatic pressure.
- Plan territory and culture ahead of time: capital, cropper, oases, and expansion slots determine your mid-game trajectory.
- Endgame objectives require early preparation: logistics, defense, timing, and alliance coordination are decisive.
