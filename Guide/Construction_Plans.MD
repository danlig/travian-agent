# Construction Plans

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000068050-construction-plans
Sezione origine: Advanced Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

Construction Plans are special artefacts required to build and upgrade a World Wonder (WW).
Without them, it’s impossible to complete the Wonder and win the server.

### When and Where Construction Plans Appear

The Natar Plan Villages spawn at a predefined time after the start of each server.
An in-game announcement will notify all players when the plans have appeared.

Each Natar Plan Village holds one World Wonder Construction Plan, and they appear near specific coordinates on the map.

Important details:
- Actual spawn coordinates can differ slightly (by ±1–2 fields).
- If the exact location is blocked, the plan spawns in the nearest available free field, which may sometimes be farther away on active servers.
- The plans may therefore appear at slightly different spots from one server to another.

Example:
If the target location is (-18|31), the actual spawn could be at (-17|30) or (-18|34), depending on where space is available.

### How to Capture a Construction Plan

Construction Plans are captured just like artefacts — from the Natars or other players who already possess them.

To successfully capture a plan:
- You must have a Treasury level 10 in the attacking village.
- The Treasury in the target village (where the plan is currently stored) must be destroyed during the attack.
- Your Hero must be part of the attacking army and survive the battle after the Treasury is destroyed.
Only the hero can carry a World Wonder Construction Plan.

These steps can be performed by one or multiple players. For example:
- One player can clear the defense,
- Another can destroy the Treasury,
- A third can send their hero from a village with a level 10 Treasury to collect the plan.

### How Construction Plans Work

To start building a World Wonder:
- The player who owns the WW village must hold at least one active Construction Plan.

To build beyond level 49:
- A second Construction Plan must be held by another player within the same alliance.
- Both plans must be active artefacts (inactive plans will not work).

Note:
If the same player (avatar) holds two plans, they count as one, not two.
Two different players in the same alliance must each hold an active plan for construction to continue past level 49.

## Implicazioni strategiche
- Gestisci risorse, produzione e capienza come vincoli principali: spendi prima di raccogliere reward grandi e usa NPC merchant solo quando sblocca timing o previene overflow significativo.
- L'eroe e gli oggetti sono acceleratori: avventure sicure, equip coerente con obiettivo corrente e salute preservata per evitare downtime.
- Ogni scelta militare deve avere ritorno chiaro: profitto, difesa, conquista, demolizione mirata o pressione diplomatica.
- Diplomazia e informazione riducono rischio: controlla messaggi/report, comunica naturalmente e costruisci affidabilita.
- Pianifica territorio e cultura in anticipo: capitale, cropper, oasi e slot espansione decidono la traiettoria mid game.
- Gli obiettivi endgame richiedono preparazione anticipata: logistica, difesa, timing e coordinamento alleanza sono decisivi.
