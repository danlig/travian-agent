# Troops Speed Increase & Tournament Square

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000068300-troops-speed-increase-tournament-square
Sezione origine: Advanced Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

Troop movement speed can be increased in several ways. These bonuses can come from hero items, artefacts, and the Tournament Square building. All speed bonuses are stackable, but some work differently depending on distance.

## Hero Items That Increase Speed

### Pennants
- Increase troop movement speed between your own villages.
- Bonus applies only when the hero travels with the troops.
- Bonus range: 30%–50% faster.

### Standards
- Increase troop movement speed between alliance or confederacy members.
- Bonus applies only when the hero travels with the troops.
- Bonus range: 15%–25% faster.
- Hero Left Hand Items

### Boots
- Increase troop movement speed after the first 20 fields of travel.
- Bonus applies only when the hero travels with the troops.
- Bonus range: 25%–75% faster.
- The first 20 fields are always traveled without this bonus.
- Hero Armour

## Artefact Bonus

### Titan’s Boots
- Large artefact: 1.5× speed
- Small or unique artefact: 2× speed

These bonuses apply regardless of distance.
- Artefacts
- Artefact Effects

## Tournament Square

The Tournament Square increases movement speed after the first 20 fields.
- Each level adds +20% speed (multiplicative after 20 fields).
- At level 20, the total bonus is +500%, meaning troops move 5× their base speed after the first 20 fields.

Tournament Square requires Rally Point level 15.

## How Multiple Bonuses Stack

Speed bonuses follow specific stacking rules:
- Boots and Tournament Square bonuses add together, but only apply after 20 fields.
- All other bonuses (Pennants, Standards, Artefacts) multiply with each other.
- Final speed is calculated by multiplying all applicable bonuses.

### Example
- Boots of the Mercenary: +25%
- Tournament Square level 10: +200%
- Artefact bonus: ×2
- Standard: +20%

First 20 fields:
Base speed × 2 (artefact) × 1.2 (standard) = 2.4× base speed

After 20 fields:
Base speed × 2 (artefact) × 1.2 (standard) × (1 + 0.25 + 2) = 5.65× base speed

## Implicazioni strategiche
- Gestisci risorse, produzione e capienza come vincoli principali: spendi prima di raccogliere reward grandi e usa NPC merchant solo quando sblocca timing o previene overflow significativo.
- L'eroe e gli oggetti sono acceleratori: avventure sicure, equip coerente con obiettivo corrente e salute preservata per evitare downtime.
- Ogni scelta militare deve avere ritorno chiaro: profitto, difesa, conquista, demolizione mirata o pressione diplomatica.
- Diplomazia e informazione riducono rischio: controlla messaggi/report, comunica naturalmente e costruisci affidabilita.
- Pianifica territorio e cultura in anticipo: capitale, cropper, oasi e slot espansione decidono la traiettoria mid game.
- Gli obiettivi endgame richiedono preparazione anticipata: logistica, difesa, timing e coordinamento alleanza sono decisivi.
