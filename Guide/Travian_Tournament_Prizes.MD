# Travian Tournament - Prizes

Fonte: https://support.travian.com/en/support/solutions/articles/7000093212-travian-tournament-prizes
Sezione origine: Advanced Gameplay

## Cosa insegna

The Travian Tournament rewards not only glory, but also valuable prizes across both the Qualification Round and the Finals. Below is an overview of all prize categories and how you can earn them.

## Finals Prizes

### Main Prize

The holder of the winning Wonder of the World receives:
- A digital voucher worth €4,000
- A unique trophy

### Other World Wonders

To qualify for a prize, the Wonder of the World must reach at least level 1 by the end of the Finals.

Rewards based on ranking:
- Ranks 2–5: Digital voucher worth €2,000
- Ranks 6–13: Digital voucher worth €1,000

### Victory Is a Team Effort – Alliance Rewards

Every member of the alliance that owns one of the top Wonders of the World receives Gold vouchers:
- Best Wonder of the World: 1,600 Gold
- Second-best Wonder of the World: 1,000 Gold
- Third-best Wonder of the World: 600 Gold

### Player Rankings

Players who reach the top 20 in offense, defense, or population rankings receive:
- A Travian Gold voucher worth 1,600 Gold

## Qualification Round Prizes

### Wonder of the World Rewards
- Holder of the winning Wonder of the World: 1,600 Gold voucher
- All owners of a Wonder of the World that reaches level 1 or higher at the end of the Qualification round receive:

1 entry key for the Tournament Finals

All owners of a Wonder of the World that reaches level 1 or higher at the end of the Qualification round receive:
- 1 entry key for the Tournament Finals

### Player Rankings
- Top 20 in offense, defense, or population:

1,200 Gold voucher

Top 20 in offense, defense, or population:
- 1,200 Gold voucher
- Top 100 in offense or defense:

1 entry key for the Finals

Top 100 in offense or defense:
- 1 entry key for the Finals

### Survivors
- Any player with 10 or more villages at the end of the Qualification world receives:

1 entry key for the Finals

Any player with 10 or more villages at the end of the Qualification world receives:
- 1 entry key for the Finals

### Winning in Multiple Categories

Each player can receive only one reward per game world.
If a player qualifies for multiple prizes, they receive the reward corresponding to their best performance.

## Implicazioni strategiche
- Diplomazia e informazione riducono rischio: controlla messaggi/report, comunica naturalmente e costruisci affidabilita.
- Pianifica territorio e cultura in anticipo: capitale, cropper, oasi e slot espansione decidono la traiettoria mid game.
- Gli obiettivi endgame richiedono preparazione anticipata: logistica, difesa, timing e coordinamento alleanza sono decisivi.
